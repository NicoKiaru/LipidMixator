SELECTION OF LIPID MIXES

What's this ?

You'll find in this page a few lipid mix that are of general (and personal) interest in the field of membrane biology and biophysics. For each mix, you can download the corresponding JSON file and open it in this page.
Phase transition with Sphingomyelin - DOPC - Cholesterol

Mix making phase transition if prepared above the phase transition (55°C is ok). The rhodamine lipid stains the liquid disordered phase.

Escrt-III patch growth

Lipid membrane made to create supported membranes on which Escrt-III proteins from yeast are able to polymerize. Contains charged lipids and a red dye for fluorescence microscopy.
Work by the Aurélien Roux lab .
